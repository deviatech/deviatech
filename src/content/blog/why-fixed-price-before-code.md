Most software shops in Lahore quote by the hour. That sounds fair until you realize it means **you**, the client, carry all the risk — if the estimate is wrong, you pay for it.

We do the opposite. Every project starts with a short discovery call, then a written scope and a fixed price before a single line of code is written. If something changes mid-project, we re-scope it explicitly rather than quietly running the meter.

## Why this works for local businesses

Most of our clients aren't technical, and they're used to negotiating a price up front for physical goods and services. A software quote should work the same way — you should know exactly what you're paying before you commit.

## What it means for you

- No surprise invoices.
- A written scope you can hold us to.
- If the project turns out to need more than expected, that's our estimation mistake to absorb — not yours to pay for.
