We get this question a lot: "Should I get a custom-built store, or is Shopify enough?" The honest answer is usually Shopify — if you're selling physical products with a fairly standard buying flow.

## When Shopify is the right call

- You're selling products (clothing, food, gifts) with standard checkout needs.
- You need to launch in days, not months.
- You want to manage inventory, discounts, and orders yourself without engineering help.

## When a custom build makes sense

- Your core value is a specific workflow or feature Shopify doesn't support out of the box (marketplaces, subscriptions with custom logic, multi-vendor logistics).
- You're building a product, not a store — something investors or early users need to test a hypothesis, not just buy an item.

If you're not sure which one you are, that's exactly what a discovery call is for — we'll tell you honestly, even if the honest answer is the cheaper option.
